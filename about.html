All About Vitiligo

Vitiligo is a skin condition where patches of skin lose their pigment. It affects people of all skin types, though it may be more noticeable in those with darker skin. It occurs when melanocytes the cells responsible for skin pigmentation are destroyed by the immune system or stop functioning.

Vitiligo is not contagious or life-threatening, but it can have emotional and psychological impacts, especially when experienced at a young age or in highly visible areas. While there is no cure, treatment options include light therapy, topical steroids, and cosmetic cover-ups.

The most important part of living with vitiligo is embracing yourself understanding that beauty is not defined by symmetry or color, but by confidence, resilience, and authenticity.
