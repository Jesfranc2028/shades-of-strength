Beauty in every mark.

Each patch on Rozie’s skin is a brushstroke on a masterpiece. Her story is not about hiding it’s about honoring. Every mark speaks of endurance, acceptance, and pride. She teaches her child that scars and changes don’t take away from beauty they add to it. Because true beauty doesn’t ask to be erased. It asks to be seen, fully and truly.
